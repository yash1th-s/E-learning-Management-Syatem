BRINGING FREE AND OPEN LEARNING TO ANYONE INTRESTED TO LEARN CS


We focus on building sustainable Computer Science courese with a proven curriculum for high school students by pairing school teachers with CS grade students and tech professionals in the local community.


A simple mission to close the Computer Science gap in high schools and local communities.

We partner with schools/ organizations around the world to make sustainable CS curiculum accessible for high school students in our local communities.
